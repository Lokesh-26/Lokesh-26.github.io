## Cyber-Physische Systeme (CPS)

**Institution:** TU Dortmund University  
**Semester:** Summer Semester  
**Role:** Lecturer & Exercise Supervisor  
**Language:** English

This course covers the fundamentals and applications of **cyber-physical systems** — systems that tightly integrate computational and physical processes. Topics include real-time systems, embedded computing, sensor-actuator integration, AI-driven control, and robotics applications in industrial and logistics contexts.

I deliver lectures and conduct exercise sessions, guiding students through theoretical concepts and hands-on exercises involving AI and robot systems.

**Course topics include:**
- Introduction to cyber-physical systems and their architectures
- Real-time computing and embedded systems
- Sensor fusion and perception for CPS
- AI and machine learning for autonomous systems
- Robotics integration in CPS (mobile robots, manipulation)
- Industrial applications: logistics, manufacturing, digital twins

### Multi-Object Tracking with FoundationPose — M.Sc. Thesis

**Student:** [Karpaga Ganesh Venkata Krishnan](https://www.linkedin.com/in/karpaga-ganesh-v/){:target="_blank"}  
**Institution:** TU Dortmund University  
**Status:** Successfully submitted  
**Project page:** [FoundationFastGluePose](https://ganesh8888.github.io/FoundationFastGluePose/){:target="_blank"}

Extended the FoundationPose 6D pose estimation framework to **multi-object tracking** in robotic grasping scenarios. The work built on the single-object pipeline from my own Master's thesis and scaled it to handle multiple objects simultaneously, contributing to the broader research on high-speed robotic perception at FLW, TU Dortmund.
