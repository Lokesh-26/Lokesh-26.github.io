Valkyrie is a bio-inspired **quadruped robot** designed and built as my Bachelor's thesis at the Hindustan Institute of Technology and Science. The project aimed to create a robot capable of stable locomotion on uneven terrain, inspired by the movement patterns of four-legged animals — contributing to advances in search-and-rescue and industrial inspection robotics.

**Key contributions:**

- Designed and developed a **bio-inspired quadruped robot** capable of mimicking animal gait movements, including trot, walk, and crawl.
- Developed gait generation using **Particle Swarm Optimization (PSO)** to find optimal leg trajectories and step sequences ({% cite srinivas2021valkyrie %}).
- Achieved locomotion stability by computing **support polygons** and performed full structural analysis using **ANSYS** to validate the chassis design.
- Secured an **Indian Patent** (No. 202241058991) for the autonomous quadruped robot system.
