**Period:** January – May 2020

**Tools:** Webots, Python, MATLAB

---

This project developed an **autonomous cost-effective robotic exploration and mapping system** for disaster reconnaissance scenarios, using swarm intelligence and heuristic navigation to maximize coverage in unknown and hazardous environments ({% cite seenu2022autonomous %}).

**Key contributions:**

- Developed **autonomous behavior-based robots** using heuristic methods for efficient deployment and navigation in disaster zones.
- Implemented advanced **search algorithms** for navigation, mapping, and path planning optimized for disaster management scenarios.
- **Simulated a swarm of robots** in Webots to ensure system integrity and performance across various disaster situations.
- The system demonstrated cost-effective deployment strategies suitable for real-world use in search-and-rescue operations.
