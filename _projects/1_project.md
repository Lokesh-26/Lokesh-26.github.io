This project is the outcome of my Master's thesis at the Lehrstuhl für Förder- und Lagerwesen (FLW), TU Dortmund. The work targets high-speed robotic grasping for mobile robots operating in logistics environments, where accurate and fast 6D pose estimation of objects is essential.

**Key contributions:**

- Developed an optimized **data acquisition and preprocessing pipeline**, significantly reducing manual annotation effort and accelerating training for 6D pose estimation models.
- Benchmarked a custom dataset using the state-of-the-art **FoundationPose** 6D pose estimation model, evaluating accuracy and performance with the **BOP toolkit**.
- Designed a **keypoint-based 6D pose estimation pipeline** for known objects, improving inference speed for real-time robotic grasping applications.

The project produced the **MR6D dataset and benchmark**, later published at the ICCV 2025 Workshop ({% cite Gouda_2025_ICCV %}).
